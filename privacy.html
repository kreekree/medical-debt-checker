Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Overview

WageGarnishmentLaws.com ("we", "us", "our") is a free informational tool. This privacy policy explains what information we collect when you visit this site, how we use it, and your choices.

This site does not require registration, collect personal information, or store any data you enter into the garnishment checker tool. All tool calculations run in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers.

Information We Collect

Analytics data. We use Google Analytics 4 and Umami to understand how visitors use this site. This includes pages visited, time on site, approximate geographic location (country/region level), device type, and referring URL. This data is aggregated and anonymized — we cannot identify individual visitors.

Tool inputs. When you use the garnishment checker, your state selection, debt type, debt amount, and employment type are processed entirely in your browser. This data is never transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere.

Contact form submissions. If you use the contact form, your name, email address, and message are transmitted to our email service provider (Formspree) to deliver your message to us. We retain contact submissions for up to 12 months.

Cookies

This site uses cookies in the following ways:

  • Google Analytics cookies: Used to distinguish users and track sessions. These cookies collect anonymized usage data. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Umami: A privacy-first analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information.
  • Advertising cookies (Ezoic): We use Ezoic for advertising. Ezoic may place cookies to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can manage these preferences through Ezoic's consent management platform.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics 4 — usage analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Umami Analytics — privacy-first analytics, no personal data collected
  • Ezoic — advertising platform. Ezoic Privacy Policy
  • Formspree — contact form processing. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Tailwind CSS CDN / Google Fonts — may log your IP address as part of serving these resources

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you.
